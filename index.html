Employee Turnover Risk Analysis

Duration: July 2025 – September 2025

Business Problem

The organisation needed to understand which employees were at risk of leaving and what factors contributed most to turnover.

Data Environment

HRIS employee records, absenteeism data, performance ratings, and tenure information.

Approach & Methodology

Used Python for data cleaning and exploratory analysis, and built a predictive model inside Power BI to identify turnover risk patterns.

Key Insight

High absenteeism, low engagement scores, and short tenure were strong indicators of potential turnover.

HR Business Impact

Helps HR proactively identify at-risk employees and improve retention strategies before attrition occurs.
